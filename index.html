NEOVERSE

This is a fantastic game that I Just disocovered a few weeks ago, essentially you must build a deck of cards and fight enemies. There are 3 characters to choose from in this game basically a fighter, summoner, and paladin. Each with unique cards and abilities. The first of the three focuses on shooting and decay skills. The second faith and vamparism although I've found that the two don't work harmoniously together. The third summons an ally for ferocious attacks.

NOITA

This game is really cool. I havent had the chance to delve very deeply into it. No pun intended. But every pixel can be burned, frozen, destroyed, melted, drained, or alteredd in some way shape or form it is truely incredible. From what I can tell you play a little wizard guy and the entire goal of the game is to go as deep down as you possibly can witht the enemides getting stronger and stronger as you go.

Port Royale 3

This game is all about trading and ship battles. Its incredibley fun to organize a fleet of ships and command them to make money for you while you go around doing the same. The game takes place in the Carribean and I think its fair to note that you are also able to take ownership over the various islands and develop them into whatever you want. Meaning manufacturing goods etc. There is a campaign and a free play mode. Multiplayer is also a viable option.

Tainted Grail

This game is another deck builder with over 8 classes to choose from. The classes are unloced simply by playing and ever single class has a very different play style from summoner, to blood mage, to setinal, to berserker and more. The only catch is you prettyt much have to beat the game in one go meaning if you kill the first boss and then die to a mob in the second area you have to start over and re-kill the first boss again. Once you've figured out how to do it though with your particual class it isn't a big issue. Annoyoing but not really a problem.
