Book of Morgahn

Detailed Guide - GrumpyGreen.com (Book of Morgahn)

Summary:

  • Book 1: +25k maximum Agris Fever points and a +50% drop rate for junk loot (giving a total +150% drop rate).
  • Book 2: +25k max Agris Fever Points and an additional 5k Agris Fever Point recovery each day.
    • +150% junk loot
    • +20,000 points/day
    • Max Agris Fever 100k Points

    Book 1:
    Chapter 1:
  • +5,000 Max Agris
    • After completing the quest to talk to Edan, from the Black Spirit, interact with the Altar of Agris.
    Chapter 2:
  • +5,000 Max Agris
    • Give Fruit of Enchantment x10, Bear Meat x30, Wheat x100, to Alter of Agris.
    Chapter 3:
  • +5,000 Max Agris
    • Enter the hidden place in the costal cave of Velia, enter the cave near the node manager and look for ablue light, it will be in and on the northern wall of the cave.
    Chapter 4:
  • +10,000 Max Agris
    • Go to the hidden place in Cron Castle (see image in detailed guide).
    Chapter 5:
  • Junk Item Drop +50%
    • Talk with Alustin to combine stone tablet fragments into one the speak with Edan back at the stone chamber.

    Book 2:
    Chapter 6:
  • +5,000 Max Agris
    • Defeat 1,000 Sausan Garrison mobs
    Chapter 7:
  • +2,500 Daily Agris recovery
    • Defeat 1,500 Crescent Shrine mobs
    Chapter 8:
  • +10,000 Max Agris
    • Defeat 2,000 Sherekhan warriors
    Chapter 9:
  • +2,500 Daily Agris recovery
    • Defeat 2,000 Manshaum mobs
    Chapter 10:
  • +10,000 Max Agris
    • Defeat 1,500 Stars End mobs
