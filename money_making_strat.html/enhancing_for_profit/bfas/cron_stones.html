Cron Stones

Although loathed by most they are a necessary part of enhancing. They are given as quest rewards, rift rewards, etc. They can also be purchased from certain blacksmiths for 2,000,000 silver a pop. By using cron stones on weapons/armor you prevent the grade from dropping down one on DUO and higher. With accessories there is still a chance of dropping but the item wont be destroyed. The cost of this protection ranges greatly up to and over 1k stones.
